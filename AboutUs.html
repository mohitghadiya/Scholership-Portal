About Us

Empowering students through scholarships and financial aid

Our Mission

We aim to make education accessible by connecting students with scholarship opportunities worldwide. Our platform helps students find financial aid to achieve their academic dreams.

How does Portal Work:

This portal allows fund providers to create their own education finance scheme and manage it through their own foundation or through a portal empanelled trust/ foundation. Through portal the fund providers are able to;

  1. Create and manage scholarship schemes
  2. Manage student application
  3. View/access student profile for decision making
  4. Set-up different user profiles
